My Gaming Journey in Valorant

Why is Valorant so popular?

Most of Valorant's success can be attributed to its unique story, gameplay, and characters, and the creators continue to capitalize on these features. Furthermore, Valorant features a diverse set of playable agents and maps from all over the world, which speaks to its global player base.
